📋 Overview

12 weeks · 4 sessions per week · ~1 hour each · 4 hours total weekly commitment. Strength cycle: Legs / Push / Pull / Legs.

📅 Ideal Schedule

Day 1 – Legs · Day 2 – Push · Day 3 – Pull · Day 4 – Rest · Day 5 – Legs · Days 6–7 – Rest

🏋️ 10×10 Rep Scheme

100 reps in 20 mins. New set every 2 mins. Must be 10 uninterrupted reps — 5+5 with a rest doesn't count. Fail at set 8 = your score. Beat it next time.

💀 Death By Calories

Min 1: 1 cal. Min 2: 2 cals. Keep going until you can't complete the number in the minute. Record your minute.

📱 Technique Videos

Tap ▶ WATCH on any exercise to see Ian's linked technique video. For squats and deadlifts — film yourself from the side at least once a week.

⚖️ Weight Selection

Go lighter than you think Week 1. Sets 1–4 should feel easy. By sets 8–10 you'll be glad you were conservative.
